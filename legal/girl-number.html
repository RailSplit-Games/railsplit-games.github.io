Who We Are

The App is published by RailSplit Games LLC ("we", "us", or "our"). If you have any questions about this Policy or your personal information, you can reach us at privacy@railsplitgames.com.

Girl Number, Safe Phone is a personal-privacy phone utility. It gives you your own unique phone number that you can share instead of your real number. When someone calls or texts that number, calls are forwarded to your real phone for a limited time window, and incoming text messages are stored in the App so you can read them later.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the service:

  • Account information. Your email address and authentication credentials, which you provide when you create an account.
  • Your real ("forwarding") phone number. The number of your own phone, which you provide so that calls to your assigned number can be forwarded to you.
  • Your assigned virtual number. The unique phone number we provision and assign to your account.
  • Inbound call metadata. For calls placed to your assigned number, we record the caller's number, the time of the call, and the call duration. We do not record or listen to the audio of your calls.
  • Inbound text messages. The content of SMS/MMS messages sent to your assigned number, along with related metadata (sender number, timestamp), stored so you can read them in the App.
  • Subscription status. Whether your subscription is active, in a trial, or expired. Payments are processed by Apple; we never receive or store your card or payment details.
  • Diagnostic and usage information. Basic product-analytics events (such as which screens you use) and crash/error reports that help us keep the App stable. Where possible, this information is kept separate from the content of your calls and messages.

We do not ask for or access your device contacts, photos, location, or microphone for the purpose of this service, and we do not sell your personal information.

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provision and maintain your assigned phone number;

  • Authenticate you and secure your account;

  • Manage your subscription, trials, and entitlements;
  • Diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and understand how the App is used so we can improve it;
