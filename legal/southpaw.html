Who We Are

Southpaw is published by RailSplit Games LLC. The App shows you a live feed of free left-handed items sourced from Freegle, a public third-party giveaway platform. You can browse, filter, bookmark, and share listings. If you subscribe to Southpaw Plus, you can also receive push alerts when new matching items appear.

If you have any questions about this policy or your information, contact us at privacy@railsplitgames.com.

Information We Collect

We designed Southpaw to need as little personal information as possible. Here is what is and isn't collected:

  • On-device data. Your bookmarks, filter preferences, and "last seen" state are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or receive this information.
  • Push notification data (only if you enable alerts). If you turn on new-item alerts, our push provider (OneSignal) generates a push token tied to your device and stores your alert preferences (such as the search terms or categories you want to be notified about) so we can send you relevant notifications. To match new listings against your preferences and deliver alerts, our server-side poller stores these alert preferences and the associated push token.
  • Subscription status. If you purchase Southpaw Plus, we receive your subscription status (for example, whether your subscription is active) from Apple through our subscription manager (RevenueCat). We never receive or store your credit card or payment card numbers â€” those are handled entirely by Apple.
  • Analytics and crash data. We collect basic product analytics (such as which features are used and general app-usage events) and crash and error reports to keep the App working and to improve it. This data is collected through PostHog (analytics) and Sentry (crash reporting) and may include device and app information such as device model, operating system version, app version, and an anonymous or pseudonymous identifier.

What we do NOT collect: We do not collect your Freegle account credentials, and we are not affiliated with Freegle. We only display items that are already publicly listed on Freegle. We do not ask for your name, email address, mailing address, or contact list to use the App. We do not sell your personal information.
