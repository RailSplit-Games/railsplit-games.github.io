Privacy Policy

Effective date / Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RailSplit Games LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the DoorGhost mobile application (the "App"). We have designed DoorGhost to collect as little information as possible. Please read this policy carefully so you understand what we do and, just as importantly, what we do not do.

Who We Are

DoorGhost is published by RailSplit Games LLC. DoorGhost is a parody and novelty entertainment app: you place pretend food "orders" that are never real, watch a fake tracking animation, and receive a fake "delivered" payoff. No real food is ever ordered, prepared, delivered, or paid for through the App. If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at privacy@railsplitgames.com.

Information We Collect

DoorGhost is built to work without an account and without a backend server of our own. Here is what that means in practice:

  • On-device data only (we never receive it). Your pretend order history, stats, and in-app activity are stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage (SwiftData). This information stays on your device. It is not transmitted to us and we have no way to access it.
  • No account and no personal identifiers. The App does not ask you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, contacts, location, or any real delivery information. Because no real food is ordered, there is nothing real to deliver and no address to collect.
  • No payment card information. We never collect, receive, or store credit or debit card numbers. All purchases are handled by Apple (see "How We Share Information" below).
  • Subscription status. If you purchase a subscription, we receive a record of whether your subscription is active, along with related purchase information (such as the product purchased and renewal status) through our subscription processor. This does not include your payment card details.
  • Anonymous crash and usage analytics. To keep the App working and to understand how features are used, we collect basic, anonymous diagnostic information such as crash reports and aggregate usage events (for example, that a screen was opened or a feature was used). This information is not used to identify you personally.

We do not knowingly collect any sensitive personal information, and we do not sell your personal information.

How We Use Information

We use the limited information described above only to:

  • Provide and operate the App and its novelty features;
  • Verify and manage subscription status so that paid novelty content is unlocked correctly;
  • Diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve stability and performance; and
  • Understand, in aggregate and anonymously, how the App is used so we can improve it.

We do not use your information for advertising profiles, and we do not build marketing profiles about you.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share limited information only with the service providers that make the App function, and only as needed for the purposes above:

  • Apple. Apple processes all payments and manages subscriptions purchased through the App Store. We never receive your card numbers. Apple's handling of your payment information is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat. We use RevenueCat to manage and verify subscription status (for example, whether your subscription is active). RevenueCat processes subscription and purchase information on our behalf.
  • Sentry. We use Sentry to collect and review crash and error reports so we can fix problems in the App.
  • PostHog. We use PostHog to collect anonymous, aggregate analytics about how the App is used.

We may also disclose information if required to do so by law, or to protect our legal rights, but given how little we collect, there is very little to disclose. If our business is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this policy.

Data Retention

Because your pretend order history and stats are stored only on your device, you control that data directly, and it is removed when you delete the App or clear its data. Subscription status and anonymous crash and analytics data are retained by our service providers for only as long as needed for the purposes described above and in accordance with their standard retention practices. We keep information no longer than necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information about you, and to opt out of certain processing. Because the App stores your activity on your device and we do not maintain accounts, the most direct way to exercise these rights is:

  • Access and correction. Your in-app data lives on your device and is visible to you within the App. You can email us at privacy@railsplitgames.com with any questions about the limited data our processors hold.
  • Deletion. You can delete your on-device data at any time by deleting the App. To request deletion of any subscription or anonymous diagnostic data associated with your use, email us at privacy@railsplitgames.com and we will work with our processors to honor your request where applicable.
  • Opt-out. You can request that we limit non-essential analytics collection by contacting us at the email above.

To make any request, email privacy@railsplitgames.com. We will respond consistent with applicable law. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited information involved in the App. Your in-app activity stays on your device under the protections provided by your device and operating system, and the limited data handled by our processors is protected using industry-standard safeguards. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

DoorGhost is intended for users aged 17 and older. The App is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information to us, please contact us at privacy@railsplitgames.com and we will take appropriate steps to address it.
